Welcome to Accessibility for Everyone.

We believe in the power of accessibility and its critical importance within our everyday lives. Whether you need closed caption programming to watch your favourite TV Show or Movies, or on-screen lyrics to sing along to your favourite songs, accessibility is present even if you don't notice it!


One of the most crucial but often neglected components of accessibility is font size.
To toggle between font sizes, click the size you're most comfortable reading for your eyesight.

Video Files with Closed Captioning

Many people prefer to use closed captionings on their TV shows or movies to better understand context and be more aware of character names and places. However, closed captions (CC) and subtitles provide dialogue, sounds, and location changes for those experiencing hard of hearing.
